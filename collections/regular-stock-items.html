About Us

HP Imports Ltd. is a British confection and grocery importer, serving the following areas in Canada:

The lower mainland of British Columbia, North Vancouver to Chilliwack to Whistler, Clayburn, Burnaby, Richmond, Langley, Abbotsford, and Nelson.

Vancouver Island, including Ladysmith, Nanaimo, Parksville, Qualicum Beach, and Oceanside.

Red Deer, Calgary, Edmonton, St. Albert, and surrounding areas in Alberta.

Regina, Saskatchewan.

Contact us to inquire about carrying our products.
